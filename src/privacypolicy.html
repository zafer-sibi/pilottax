Services: We are committed to making your total experience a positive one. Upon entering your information, we will transmit your tax return information to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS); a process known as "e-file". We do not, however, guarantee that the IRS will accept your return. Although we take certain steps to ensure the accuracy of your return, it may be rejected for a variety of reasons, some of which are beyond our control. With this service, you are filing "self-prepared" taxes. We do not offer tax advice and make no guarantees or representations regarding the quality or accuracy of the data you enter.

Upon using our services, you will be prompted to disclose certain information about you and your business, and you will be able to store information on our Website. In order to set up an account, we ask you to create a username and password. You are responsible for any use of our services by persons to whom you intentionally or negligently allow access to your password, if any.

After registering an account, to prepare your tax return, you will be prompted to enter information to complete United States Internal Revenue Service (IRS) tax forms. This includes information such as your employer identification number, contact information (name, email, address), vehicle details, tax deduction(s), and information about your business, if any. With your consent, we will file your return with the IRS and charge you a fee for this filing service. In this case, some of your information will be sent to the IRS who will need this information to process your tax return. Billing information, such as a credit card number, will also be requested so we may process your order.

Refer-A-Business: If you choose to use our referral service to tell a friend about our site, we will ask you for your friend's name and email address. We will automatically send your friend a one-time email inviting him or her to visit the site. We do not store this information and use it for the sole purpose of sending this one-time email.

Purpose of Information Collected: We collect information from this Website for the purpose of running our business to e-file your federal excise tax. We collect and use information necessary for us to complete and file your tax returns, bill for services, and to maintain the security of our site.

Information Sharing: We share your information as required by the Internal Revenue Service in order to e-file your tax return. We send credit card information to our credit card processor for the purpose of processing your transaction. We also use a third-party service to provide some customer service features. We do not share, sell, rent, or trade personally identifiable information with third parties for their promotional purposes. We reserve the right to disclose your personally identifiable information as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on our Website or our Company.

Security of Private Information: Our site uses SSL encryption using a GoDaddy security certificate to protect sensitive information online. We take several precautions to protect user information offline. All of our users' information has restricted access. Only persons authorized by us and who require the information to perform a necessary task are granted access to personally identifiable information.

Data is transmitted to the IRS using a secure connection from our computers to IRS computers. For data transmission, we meet or exceed the security protocols established by the IRS.

Any personal and private information that is sent to you by email is designed so as not to compromise your privacy in any way. We do not store your credit card information in our database. We transmit it securely to our credit card processor, and we do not store your credit card number. By the act of not having your credit card on file, your credit card information is kept secure.

Email Policies: After you register, we may periodically contact you using your email address or phone number. Such communications would only be performed if we need to speak to you about your tax return and our e-file services used to submit that return. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to any other individuals or organizations.

Because tax filing is important, it is important that we be able to contact you about your tax return. Up until the time your tax return is accepted by the IRS, you have the option to delete your account information ("opt out"). To opt-out, please use the "Business Information" link after you log in to your account. Once your tax return has been accepted by the IRS, you may no longer delete your account information.

Log Files We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, and gather general demographic information. As an IRS requirement, your IP address and email address are linked and shared with the IRS as personal information. Other log information is kept for security reasons. That data is used to prevent or discover a hacker and will not be used for other purposes.

Cookies We use session ID cookies. These are bits of text that are temporarily set on your computer. These are used to make it easier for you to navigate our Website. For example, thanks to cookies, you do not have to log in repeatedly (from Web page to Web page). A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. We do not link the information we store in cookies to any personally identifiable information you submit while on our site. Third Parties providing services for our Web site also set cookies.

Access to Personally Identifiable Information If your personally identifiable information changes, or if you no longer desire our service, you may update your personal and tax information up until the time your tax return is accepted by the IRS. To update or change your account information, use the "Business Information" function provided after your login to your account. Once your tax return has been accepted by the IRS, no further changes can be made directly to your e-filed tax return. We lock archival data to prevent it from being edited.

Link to Other Sites This Privacy Statement applies only to information collected by this Web site. This Web site contains links to other Internet sites that are not owned or controlled by us. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the privacy practices of other Internet sites. When you leave our site please read the privacy statements of any Web site that may collect your personal information.
